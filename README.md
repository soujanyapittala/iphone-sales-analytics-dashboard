# 📌 Project Overview

This project explores iPhone sales data to uncover insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, product popularity, and regional sales trends.

The dashboard was built using Tableau Public with interactive visualizations, KPI monitoring, dashboard actions, filters, and story-based navigation to simulate real-world business intelligence reporting.

The analysis focuses on understanding:
- Product sales performance
- Revenue trends over time
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Regional sales contribution
- Payment method preferences
- Product variant popularity

---

# 📊 Dashboard Modules

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance.

### Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Total Units Sold

### Key Visualizations
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Country
- Top Selling Products
- Sales Performance Analysis

---

## 2️⃣ Product Analysis Dashboard

Focused on analyzing product performance and customer preferences.

### Key Visualizations
- Revenue by iPhone Model
- Quantity Sold by Model
- Storage Variant Analysis
- Color Popularity Analysis
- Product Revenue Contribution

---

## 3️⃣ Customer & Payment Insights

Analyzes customer purchasing behavior and transaction patterns.

### Key Visualizations
- Payment Method Analysis
- Country-wise Customer Distribution
- Monthly Purchase Trends
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Customer KPI Analysis

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- Premium iPhone variants contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Certain storage variants showed stronger customer demand patterns.
- Customer purchasing behavior varied across regions.
- Monthly sales trends indicated fluctuations in customer demand.
- Digital payment methods dominated customer transactions.
